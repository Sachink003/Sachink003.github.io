About Me

Hello! I am Sachin Kumar, an undergraduate mathematics student (BMath) majoring in Mathematical Finance and Pure Mathematics at the University of Waterloo's department of Pure mathematics in the Faculty of Mathematics..
I am an aspiring mathematics researcher and a quant (quantitative researcher/trader).

Academic Interest

My academic research interest is in the abstract field of pure mathematics, particularly in number theory (analytical and algebraic) and real analysis (lebesgue and measure theory), with it's deep application in probability theory.

Life...

I love to play squash and table tennis in my free time and like to solve puzzles which involve numbers, beautiful mathematical patterns and brain stormers. I also watch and read a lot of anime and manga, so you can hit me up for some recommendations.
